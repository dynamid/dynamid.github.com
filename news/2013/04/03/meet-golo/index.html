Meet Golo

Apr 3rd, 2013

The Dynamid group unveiled Golo last week at the Devoxx France 2013 conference.

Golo is a new dynamic language for the Java virtual machine that takes advantage of invokedynamic. It is lightweight and runs fast.

Golo will be useful to us for playing with language extensions. It provides us with a stronger JVM expertise within the Dynamid group. Of course Golo is far from being feature-complete, and we will keep pushing on the development front.

More details regarding Golo:

  • the website and documentation
  • try the language from a web browser on Google AppEngine
  • Golo on GitHub
  • the details behind the development of Golo
  • the original release announcement

Golo is made available under the permissive Apache Software Licence version 2.0, and you are highly encouraged to try it and participate!

We are looking forward to hearing what you will do with Golo.
