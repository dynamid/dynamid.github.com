Nighthacking

Nov 7th, 2012

DynaMid member Julien Ponge recorded a NightHacking session with Stephen Chin from Oracle.

Stephen was doing a tour of Europe before ending at the Devoxx conference. He had a stop in Lyon and the LyonJUG arranged the interview.

During the 1 hour informal discussion, Julien talked about bytecode manipulation on the JVM and even demoed JooFlux at one point.
